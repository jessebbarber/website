Density/Income

This data visualization displays the housing density and median household income (data from 2017 5-year American Community Survey) of the 50 largest "urban areas" (defined by the census) in the United States disaggregated by zip code. The coloring is scaled according all 50 urban areas such that the darkest color shade of red represents the 5% densest zip codes within all 50 urban areas. For a fullscreen version, click here.

Berkeley Zoning

This map shows the land use zoning of Berkeley, CA in 1949 (left) and 2018 (right). Slide the center button left and right and hover over the map to compare the changes in land use over the years. I made this map to accompany an article that I wrote about the racist/classist origins of Berkeley's zoning code and its effects on race and class-based boundaries and the housing crisis today. You can find the article here. For a fullscreen version, click here.
